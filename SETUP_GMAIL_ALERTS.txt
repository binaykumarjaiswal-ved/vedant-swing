VEDANT SWING — GMAIL EMAIL ALERTS SETUP
=======================================

Emails (if configured):
  - Morning research report
  - Price alert triggers

STEP 1 — Create a Gmail for alerts (optional dedicated account)
STEP 2 — Enable 2-Step Verification
STEP 3 — Create App Password: https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords

STEP 4 — Secrets (never paste in chat)

  Render / GitHub:
    EMAIL_ENABLED = true
    EMAIL_FROM    = your-alerts-gmail@gmail.com
    EMAIL_TO      = your-personal-email@gmail.com
    EMAIL_APP_PASSWORD = 16-char app password

  Local: copy secrets.example.env → secrets.env

STEP 5 — Optional auto-set secrets
  python scripts/setup_email_secrets.py

STEP 6 — Test
  python scripts/test_email.py

Subjects you may receive:
  [Vedant Swing] Morning Research YYYY-MM-DD
  [Vedant Swing] Price Alert ...
