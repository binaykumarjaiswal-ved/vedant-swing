VEDANT SWING          — App name
↻ Refresh             — Reloads market mood, evening scan, watchlist,
                        paper portfolio, journal, and alerts

Subtitle line         — Shows: Watchlist count · Active alerts · Time (IST)

Market pill (green/   — Nifty market mood for last 20 trading days
red/amber)              Examples: "Nifty BULLISH · 20d +3.98%"
                        BULLISH = index trending up
                        BEARISH = index trending down
                        NEUTRAL = sideways / mixed

5 TABS:
  Home    — Search stocks, charts, evening scan, position tracker
  Watch   — Your personal stock watchlist
  Paper   — Virtual Rs.5,00,000 practice trading (no real money)
  Journal — Plan and track your real trade ideas + R:R
  Alerts  — Price above/below notifications

TAB 1 — HOME
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

── SEARCH STOCK ──
  Symbol box          — Type NSE symbol (e.g. TITAN, RELIANCE, M&M)
  Analyze button      — Runs technical analysis on that stock
  Quick chips         — One-tap analyze: TITAN, RELIANCE, TCS,
                        HDFCBANK, INFY, BAJFINANCE

── PRICE CHART (appears after Analyze) ──
  Shows candlestick chart + volume + EMA lines (like Google Finance)

  Price block (top right):
    Last price        — Current / latest close
    Change line       — Green = up, Red = down (for selected period)

  TIMEFRAME BUTTONS — Tap to change chart period:
    1D   — Today, 5-minute candles (intraday)
    5D   — Last 5 days, 15-minute candles
    1M   — 1 month, daily candles
    3M   — 3 months, daily
    6M   — 6 months, daily (default)
    1Y   — 1 year, daily
    5Y   — 5 years, weekly candles
    MAX  — Full available history, weekly

  STATS ROW (updates when you change timeframe):
    Open    — First price in that period
    High    — Highest price in period (green)
    Low     — Lowest price in period (red)
    Close   — Latest price
    Volume  — Total shares traded (K = thousands, L = lakhs, Cr = crore)
    RSI     — Relative Strength Index (below 30 oversold, above 70 overbought)

  Chart lines:
    Green/red candles — Price up / down per bar
    Volume bars       — Trading activity under price
    Purple EMA9       — Short trend (intraday / short periods)
    Blue EMA21        — Medium trend
    Orange EMA50      — Longer trend

── ANALYSIS RESULT (below chart) ──
  Symbol + index tag  — Stock name + Nifty 50 / Next 50 / Nifty 500
  Signal badge        — See "SIGNAL MEANINGS" section below
  Score bar 0–100     — Higher = stronger swing setup
  Price               — Live / latest price
  Target +3%          — Suggested swing profit target (+3% from entry)
  RSI                 — Momentum indicator
  Trend               — up / down / sideways
  EMA21 / EMA50       — Moving average levels
  Signals list        — Why the app scored this stock

  ACTION BUTTONS:
    + Watchlist       — Add stock to Watch tab (track later)
    + Alert           — Jump to Alerts tab with symbol pre-filled
    Paper Buy         — Instantly paper-buy with suggested qty/price
                        Green toast = success
                        Red toast = error (e.g. already holding stock)
    Journal           — Jump to Journal tab with entry/stop/target filled

── EVENING SWING SCAN ──
  Auto-runs at 3:45 PM IST on trading days (cloud automation)
  Scans all Nifty 500 stocks for swing setups

  Run now             — Manually trigger scan (takes a few minutes)
  Meta line           — Date · number of setups found · stocks scanned

  Strategy filter chips:
    All               — Every setup found
    Pullback 21 EMA   — Stock pulled back to 21-day average in uptrend
    Breakout          — Price breaking out of tight range with volume
    Oversold Bounce   — RSI low, possible bounce in uptrend

  Tap any stock row   — Opens full analysis on Home tab

── POSITION TRACKER ──
  Shows your ONE real tracked position (if synced from cloud bot)
  Displays: symbol, qty, average price, P&L %, sell/average signals
  Record Average      — Log that you bought more at lower price
  Record Sell         — Close the tracked position
  (Empty = no open position — use Paper tab for practice instead)

  STRONG BUY  — High score, multiple bullish signals aligned
  BUY         — Good swing setup, meets strategy rules
  WATCH       — Interesting but not full entry yet — wait
  SETUP       — Pattern forming (evening scan)
  AVOID       — Weak setup or against trend — skip

  Swing score 0–100:
    70+   — Strong candidate
    62–69 — Acceptable (app minimum for research picks)
    Below 62 — Usually filtered out of scans

3 SWING STRATEGIES (EVENING SCAN)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
1. PULLBACK 21 EMA
   Stock in uptrend pulls back near 21-day moving average.
   RSI 40–55. Good for buying the dip in a strong stock.

2. BREAKOUT
   Price breaks above 10-day consolidation range with volume.
   Good for momentum entries after a tight base.

3. OVERSOLD BOUNCE
   RSI oversold but stock still above long-term trend.
   Good for quick bounce trades (higher risk).
